Website Summary

EDHrec

EDHrec is a popular website for those in the MTG Commander commuinty. It offers a wide range of capabilitys and data from other deck lists around the world helping players understand common cards and stratagies played with the deck they are building.

Scryfall

One of, if not the most well know, MTG website. Scryfall is a website front that allows a player to search through every MTG card ever printed from Alpha to the most common release. It also offers very robust sorting and filters to help find the excact card a player is looking for.

Topdecked

Is and online deck building website that allows a player to make decks and try cards they normally wouldn't before commiting to buying the card.
