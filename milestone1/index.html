Website Summary

Images: Will come from scryfall at https://scryfall.com.

Text: Will come from myself.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

It will add a quick online way to help decide wich deck to play on game night.

Why should a site be built for this?

To allow for a more simplyfied and modular website.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to be able to access deck lists of decks I currently have in my possession. As well as give users a simple random deck selector of the decks I have.

What is the goal of the site?

To make a website that can help with orgnizing and managing my hobby.
